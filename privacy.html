Privacy Policy

Effective May 22, 2026

Rekko is a mobile app for beauty professionals and independent service providers that helps manage clients, appointments, and reminders. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

1. Data we collect

1.1 Account data

  • email address — used to sign in via email magic link, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In;
  • display name and profile picture — if provided by the auth provider;
  • Firebase unique user identifier (UID).

1.2 Professional profile

  • profession category (e.g. nails, tattoo);
  • working hours, services, prices, message templates;
  • currency, locale, time zone, week start, date format.

1.3 Client data

Data you add about your clients: names, phone numbers, notes, visit history, appointments. This data belongs to you and is used only to operate the app on your account. We do not share it with third parties and do not use it for advertising.

1.4 Technical data

  • push notification token (Firebase Cloud Messaging) — for appointment reminders;
  • anonymous usage events (e.g. screen viewed, button tapped) via Firebase Analytics;
  • device model and OS version — to diagnose compatibility issues.

1.5 Subscription data

Rekko Pro purchases are processed by RevenueCat, Inc. We receive subscription status, the platform identifier (Apple or Google), and an anonymous customer ID linked to your Firebase UID. Payment card details are handled exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play — we never see or store them.

1.6 Notifications permission

The app requests permission to send notifications. Notifications are used locally on your device to remind you about upcoming appointments and to alert you about sleeping clients. They are scheduled by the app itself and, where applicable, delivered via Firebase Cloud Messaging.

2. How we use data

  • operate your account and synchronize data across your devices;
  • send reminders about upcoming appointments and sleeping clients;
  • process and validate your Pro subscription;
  • measure aggregate, anonymous usage analytics to improve the product;
  • detect and fix technical issues.

3. Who we share data with

We do not sell your data. We rely on the following processors:

  • Google Firebase (Google LLC) — Auth, Firestore, Cloud Messaging, Analytics;
  • RevenueCat, Inc. — subscription management;
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — payment processing;
  • Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In — authentication providers (only if you choose them).

Each processor operates under its own privacy policy and applies industry-standard data protection measures.

4. Data retention and deletion

Data is retained while your account exists. When you delete your account from Settings → Delete account, all content — clients, appointments, services, templates, settings — is removed within 30 days. Encrypted backups may persist for up to 90 days for disaster recovery.

5. Your rights

Under applicable law (including GDPR for EU users) you may:

  • request a copy of your data;
  • correct inaccurate data;
  • delete your account and related data;
  • object to or restrict processing.

To exercise these rights, email xeniqu@gmail.com.

6. Children

Rekko is not intended for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from such users. If we learn that we have, we delete it.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be announced in-app or by email at least 14 days before they take effect.

8. Contact

Questions or requests: xeniqu@gmail.com.
