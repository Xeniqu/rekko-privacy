Getting started

How do I add a client?
Open the Clients tab and tap “Add client.” You only need a name; phone, Instagram, and notes are optional and can be added anytime.
How do I book an appointment?
In the Calendar tab, tap an empty time slot (or the + button), choose the client and service, set the time, and save.
Can I set recurring appointments?
Yes. When creating an appointment, choose a repeat option (e.g. every week, every two weeks, every month). Rekko keeps the series going without creating an endless list.

Reminders & client retention

How do appointment reminders work?
Rekko sends you a local notification before each appointment. You can change how far in advance (15 min to 1 day) in Settings. Reminders use your device time format.
What are “sleeping clients”?
Clients who haven’t booked in a while. Rekko surfaces them on the home screen, sorted by how much they used to spend, so you can reach out before they drift away for good.
What are message templates?
Pre-written messages with placeholders for client name, service, date, and time. When you message a client about an appointment, pick a channel (WhatsApp or SMS) and a template, and the text is filled in for you. Creating multiple named templates is a Rekko Pro feature.

Rekko Pro & subscriptions

What’s included in Rekko Pro?
Unlimited clients, revenue analytics, multiple message templates, and premium light/dark themes. The free version covers the core calendar and client list.
How does the free trial and billing work?
If a free trial is offered, it converts to a paid subscription when it ends unless you cancel at least 24 hours before. Subscriptions renew automatically through your Apple App Store account. Prices vary by region and are shown on the subscription screen before you buy.
How do I restore a purchase?
Open the subscription screen in the app and tap “Restore purchases.” Make sure you’re signed in to the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Rekko → Cancel. Cancelling stops future renewals; you keep Pro until the current period ends.
I have a promo code — where do I enter it?
On the subscription screen, tap “Have a promo code?” to open Apple’s redemption sheet.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple, not by Rekko. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.

Your data & account

Is my client data private?
Yes. Your clients and appointments are yours. We never sell your data or share it for advertising. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I delete my account and data?
In the app: Settings → Delete account. This removes your content within 30 days (encrypted backups may persist up to 90 days). You can also email us to request deletion.
Can I use Rekko in my language?
Rekko is available in many languages, including English, Russian, Ukrainian, German, French, Spanish, Portuguese, Polish, Czech, Turkish, Hindi, Japanese, Korean, Chinese and more. Change it in Settings → Language.
Will Rekko work for a salon with several chairs?
Rekko is built for one person working solo. It’s not designed for multi-staff salons or for clients to book themselves online.
